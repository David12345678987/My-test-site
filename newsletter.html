Many years ago, Youngs Brewery decided theyd spruce up one of our favourite haunts, The Half Moon in Putney. One morning, Ralph McTell who lives nearby, noticed the old mahogany bar top being thrown out. Seizing the moment, Ralph took the old bar top away for recycling and had TOM MATES create two beautiful guitars from it an electric bass and an electric six string. Ralph presented the bass to Peggy for his 50th birthday and kept the six-string for himself.
